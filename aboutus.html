  • Basic Info

    • Every year our girls participate in 2 competitions (we are in the process of choosing which 2 competitions to attend this year). While we do compete, our main events are cheering at football games.

  • Purpose

    • Our purpose is to give young girls between ages 5-14 an opportunity to learn cheer skils and form bonds with other girls their age and work as a team to perform excellent cheer and dance routines. All of our cheer coaches are volunteers and this is their way of giving back to their community!

  • Pricing

    • If you did not know, being a cheerleader is expensive! The price for new girls is roughly $600. Now don't fret because we don't collect it all at once. We have monthly payments of about $120, you can pay less or more if you'd like. We also offer scholarships to select families that suffer from financial struggles.
